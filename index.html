Instructions

Home

Create a Profile

  1. Go to "Add Profile" on the Home screen to create a list of the dietary supplements one person is taking. Or, go to the "Profiles" screen and select "Add" in the upper right corner.
  2. Enter a unique profile name and click "Save" or select "Add Dietary Supplement."

Add a Dietary Supplement

  1. Select "Add Dietary Supplement" and type in the name of the dietary supplement to track.
  2. Enter the "Amount" (e.g., 1,000).
  3. Enter the "Unit" (e.g., mg).
  4. Enter "How Often" the dietary supplement is taken (e.g., 2x Daily).
  5. Add a photo of the dietary supplement.
  6. Enter relevant information in the "Notes" field to assist with discussions with your doctor.
  7. Continue to add other dietary supplements to your profile by selecting "Add."

Email Profile Content

  1. Go to "Email Profile" from the Home screen, or select "Email" on the "Profiles" screen.
  2. Select "Email" in the upper right corner.
  3. Enter the email address of the account to which you want to send the list of dietary supplements and click "Send."

Password Protection

  1. Go to the homescreen and select Settings. Select Password Protect and enter in a 4 digit pin.
